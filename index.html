An Inquiry into the Fuel Economy of Various Cars



Fuel efficiency is one of the most talked-about points for vehicle-owners. In addition, with many countries and states openly committing to cut vehicle emissions to zero in the not-so-distant future, it seems that the electric and hybrid vehicle sectors will only continue to grow.

This interactive slideshow will take a look at data derived from https://flunky.github.io/cars2017.csv, which is an example dataset used for UIUC's CS416 Data Visualization class. It contains information about various cars in 2017, including the make of the car, the city/highway mpg, the fuel type, and engine cylinders. We will create several visualizations using this data using javascript's d3 library, in order to explore some questions around fuel efficiency such as:

  • What's the relationship between highway, city, and combined fuel efficiency?
  • Does the measurement "Miles per Gallon" even make sense for electric cars?
  • How does a car's engine affect fuel efficiency?
